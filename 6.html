Melwyn Peris ‘Yodelling King’ – Shri Melwyn Peres

He was born on 13th April 1950 at Bajpe, Mangalore Diocese and later he settled in Bejai, Mangalore. Currently He's stationed at Urwa with his family. His father is Paul and his mother is Florine D'Souza. There 3 sisters and 5 brothers and he is the eldest. His wife is Joyce and he has two children, a girl named Karishma Michelle and a boy named Karan Russel. He had my early education at Canara High School (Main), and acquired a B.A. degree through St.Aloysius Evening College, Mangalore. Later, He did a Journalism Diploma Course at Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan, Mangalore.
