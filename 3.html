Wilson Olivera A BRIGHT RISING STAR IN THE KONKAN SKIES "WILSON OLIVERA"

Here is a Konkanni Star in the making. Rising higher and higher, slow but steady everyday. His lyrics are selected and meaningful, his compositions are unique and superb, his voice is melodious and captivating. His love for Konkani is wholehearted and unparalleled. He is none other than Shri Wilson Olivera from the little known village of Padukone! Still residing in this village, without the urban facilities and opportunities for a musical career, which are available to a dweller in cities like Mangalore, Bangalore or Mumbai, he has displayed his musical prowess and other diverse talents in a stunning way and has earned his name and enviable fame
