Henry D’Souza THE ONE AND ONLY ‘SANGEETH SAMRAT’ & ‘KALA RATNA’

Once again I extend to you a warm welcome for my Do-Re-Me-Fa column. I’m sure, like my earlier musical chords you will enjoy reading this sixth chord also. In this sensational chord I would like to present a great composer, singer, conqueror of song lover’s heart, who is none other than an evergreen hero of Konkanni music and songs ‘Ye Ye Cathrina’ and ‘Ami Dhoghi Sezara’ fame ‘Sangeeth Samraat’ Sri Henry D’Souza’.

This rare jewel of Konkanni music, who never looked back since he raced and jumped first as Bombay’s craze in mid sixtees and later in other parts of India and the Gulf. His creations have been broadcasted in ALL INDIA RADIO and radio SRI LANKA and his HMV records entertained the young and old of every generations and every language groups of the world.

I must say that the Dubai music lovers are highly privileged to have in their midst this great music star with his long time singing partner Dr.Aparna Mayekar with their all time great hits at the gala show to be held on February 21, 2003 from 11 a.m. onwards at the poolside of the Al Nasr Leisurland, Dubai. St.Mary’s Association Shirva, UAE, organizes this memorable show. Internationally popular daijidubai fame Shri Walter Nandalike & Dayan D`Souza will compere this unique show and no doubt this is going to attract Konkanni music lovers in big number and undoubtedly will be a historical show.
